Temple Inn & Suites Site Plan

Camille Hunter

Overview

Purpose

The Temple Inn & Suites Website will provide a place for temple workers, patrons, and their families to learn about the services and amenities available at Temple Inn & Suites. The site will reflect the culture of the Church and have a temple oriented theme. It will have a feeling of comfort and accessibility.

Audience

The target audience will be members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and their friends and family that are willing to follow the terms and policies of Temple Inn & Suites. They will range in age from young adult to seniors. They will be seeking a place to stay for their temple trip that will reflect the feeling of the temple.

Scenarios

  • My husband and I just received our senior mission papers to be a temple worker. Where are we going to stay?
  • I’m so excited about Jane’s engagement and her decision to married in the temple. I need to find accommodations for the out of town guests.

  • I really want to go to my sister’s sealing! How am I going to find a babysitter for my kids?
  • I’m so excited for my mission. I need to find a place to stay close to the temple when I take out my endowments. I know I won’t be able to get to the temple and back in one day, since it is such a long drive. I wonder where I can find a place to stay the night before.

Typography

Heading Font: Raleway

The elegant nature of Raleway will help emphasize the connection it has with the temple oriented theme. It's sans-serif shape will help it standout against the serif body text.

Paragraph Font: Crimson Text

Crimson Text is a classic looking serif font. It has a sense of history that would represent the feeling of the temple well. It has great readability for paragraphs.

Normal paragraph example

We offer a complete package for whatever your needs are in relation to temple service and accomodations. Our shuttle runs to meet the endowment schedule and our babysitting servcies provide an afforable and safe alternative for your family.

Colored paragraph example

Plan on using our free changing room for you and your fellow patrons as you transition from traveling to the temple to actually entering the temple. We want you to feel comfortable changing and freshing up in order to focus on meaningful service. Let us take care of you.

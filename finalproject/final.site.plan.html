Millie the Learning Guide Site Plan

Camille Hunter

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website will be to provide a fun place for elementary through highschool kids to learn how to write better stories. This will be done by teaching basic parts of speech and playing a mad lib game. As well teaching students how to help their reader better experience the story by writing using the five senses.

Audience

The target audience will be 2nd through 10th graders, parents, and elementary educators. They will be looking for a website that help them or their students learn a writing skill. They will be looking for a free website that doesn't take a lot of work to navigate.

Scenarios

  • I'm bored. I wonder if I can find a mad libs for me and my younger brother to do together.
  • My 3nd grade daughter is struggling in school. Her lowest grades are in language arts. I wonder if there is a fun way to help her learn some of the concepts.
  • I need a new website to recommend to my students when they get done with their classwork a little early.
  • A 6th grade student that is working on a story for class. He wanted to get a better score on his assignment than last week, and his teacher recommended the site.

Typography

Heading Font: Architects Daughter

The sans-serif font adds character and playfulness to the website. It helps create the feel of the website that is a place for creativity and fun.

Paragraph Font: Crimson Text

Crimson Text is a classic looking serif font. It adds a great contrast to the playfulness of Architects Daughter. It has great readability for the main text of the website, as well.

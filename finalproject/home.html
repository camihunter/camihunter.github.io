Meet Mille

My name is Millie! I can't wait to teach you all of the things I have been learning. Writing is so much fun!

Why I Write

I love to write because it gives me the chance to use my imagination. I can travel to anywhere in this universe or another. I can work through challenges in my life by working through challenges with my characters. Sometimes experiences in my life are hard to process. Writing gives me an outlet to explore my emotions. If you give creative writing a try, and keep practicing, I'm sure you will love writing as much as me!

Benefits of Writing

  • Writing can help you become a better reader
  • 15-20 minutes of writing a day can reduce stress
  • Writing helps the brain develop the logical functions required for successful math and science learning
  • Writing helps improve communication skills

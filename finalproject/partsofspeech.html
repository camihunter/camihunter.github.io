Parts of Speech

Why I learned the different parts of speech...

While I was learning to write, I learned the diffferent parts of speech to help me make sure my sentence structure was correct. This is very important in proper grammar. Proper grammer can help your reader better understand what you're writing.

Let's Learn!

Noun

A noun is a person (she), place (home), or thing (flower).

Proper Noun

A proper noun is a specific person, place, or thing. Proper nouns should always begin with a capital letter.

Plural Noun

A plural noun is a more than one person (girls), place (parks), or thing (donkeys).

Verb

Verbs are words that show an action (sing), occurrence (develop), or state of being (exist). Almost every sentence requires a verb.

Adjective

An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun or a pronoun.

Adverb

An adverb is a word used to modify a verb, an adjective, or another adverb and often used to show degree, manner, place, or time

Sentence Example

Harry watched the irridescent beetle slowly dance around the shallow pool.
